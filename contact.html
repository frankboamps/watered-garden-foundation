FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Watered Garden Foundation?
Watered Garden Foundation is an international non-profit organization active in over 100 countries. We give children in the U.S. and around the world a healthy start in life, the opportunity to learn, and protection from harm. When a crisis occurs, we are among the first to respond and the last to leave.
Why should I donate to Watered Garden Foundation?
Your gift, pooled with other support, helps us deliver critical aid to children and families around the world and within the US — during emergencies and through our long-term programs. Every dollar is deployed with care and accountability.
Who do I contact if I have a question about my donation?
Please contact our Supporter Experience team at info@wateredgardenfoundation.org. We are happy to address any questions or concerns about your donation promptly.
How do I apply to volunteer?
We are delighted that you are interested in helping children and families in need. Please use the contact form above or email us directly to apply. We welcome all who share our passion for meaningful, lasting change.
